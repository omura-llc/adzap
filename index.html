Ad Zap

Ad blocking for Safari

Summary

Ad Zap is an extension designed to block a handpicked array of ad domains on Safari for iOS.

Goals

  • Block most ads and trackers using a single extension.
  • Don't slow down page loads.
  • Preserve user privacy.

Installation

Ad Zap is now available on the App Store.

Beta versions are available on TestFlight.
